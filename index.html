About

As a young Korean actress, Kim So Hyun is making her talent known all over the world. She began her career at the age of seven in 2006. Thanks to her important roles in 해를 품은 달 Moon Embracing the Sun in 2012 and 보고 싶다 Missing you in 2013, she gained a lot of public interest. Her first lead role was in 후아유: 학교 2015 Who Are You: School 2015 in 2015. Since then, she has been in a huge range of dramas. This include the historical melodrama 군주-가면의 주인 The Emperor: Onwer of the Mask in 2017, the romantic comedy 라디오 로맨스 Radio Romance in 2018 and, most recently, Korean folklore 달이 뜨는 강 River Where the Moon Rises in 2021.

Kim So Hyun has been nicknamed the "Nation's Little Sister" and "Queen of Child Actresses" by the Korean media. Because of all her works, she has made herself into a top Hallyu star. With more than 10.7 million followers on her Instagram account, she is even the youngest South Korean actress to have more 10 million followers on social media. Find out everything about her life and works, see some beautiful photos, as well as hear some of her interviews.
